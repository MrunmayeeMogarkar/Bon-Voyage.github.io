Disneyland

Disneyland Paris(earstwhile Euro Disney Resort) is an entertainment resort in Chessy, France. It is located 32 km east of the centre of Paris. It comprises of two theme parks along with several additional recreational and entertainment venues. Disneyland Park is the original theme park of the complex, that opened with the resort on 12 April 1992. A second theme park, Walt Disney Studios Park, opened in 2002, 10 years after the original park. Disneyland Paris celebrated its 25th anniversary in 2017.

Other key features that attract tourists are many resort hotels along with Disney Nature Resorts, an entertainment complex, shopping and dining areas and a golf course. That is the reason Disneyland witnessed 320 million people within 25 years of its opening, making it the most visited theme park in Europe. Disneyland Paris is very significant because 1. It is the largest Disney Resort outside USA. 2. It is the second Disney Resort to open outside the USA, the first one being Disneyland Tokyo. 3. It is the only Disney resort outside of the USA to be completely owned by The Walt Disney Company.
