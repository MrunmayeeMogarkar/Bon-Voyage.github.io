Keukenhof

Keukenhof, 70 years old spring garden located in Amsterdam, is the most beautiful and popular spring garden in the world! With over 7 million tulip bulbs of 800 different varieties blooming over 32 hectares of this flower park make it a unique, spectacular and unforgettable experience for the whole family.

The flower park dedicated to a specific theme every year attracts more than one million visitors in just a few weeks of opening . The inspiration gardens, events and artworks in the park are adjusted according to the theme. The theme for 2020 was 'World of Colors'. The flower park in Holland will open for the 71th time in 2021 tentatively from 20 March till 9 May with yet unannounced theme.
