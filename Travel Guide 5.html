Amsterdam,Holland

How to reach

  1. By Air:-

    2. The primary international airport for travel is Amsterdam Airport Schiphol. There are smaller regional airports like Rotterdam, Eindhoven etc.

  2. By Train:-

    3. Amsterdam Central station is the city's major international railway hub. From here you may get the trains to the nearing countries like Austria, Denmark, France, Germany, United Kingdom etc.

  3. By Bus:-

    4. Eurolines operates international coach services from the Amsterdam Amstel railway station. Whereas IDBUS, Megabus, and Flixbus operate international routes from the Amsterdam Sloterdijk railway station.
