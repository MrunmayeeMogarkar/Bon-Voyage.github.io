Madame Tussauds

Madame Tassauds is a wax museum featuring notable celebrities. Some statues of famous indian celebrities are Narendra Modi,Shah Rukh Khan, Madhuri Dixit, Amitabh Bachchan and Sachin Tendulkar. Some statues of other famous personalities are Steven Spielberg,Taylor Swift, Michael Jackson, Cristiano Ronaldo, Queen Elizabeth II and Vincenv van Gogh.
